Exploring Network in wellington

--- Jiayue Cui ---

Since the internet has been invented from early 1960, people are constantly discovering its potential to achieve things that could not even be imaged a decades before. People can share and gain information via internet. In the other word, internet connected all of the people together. As the technology development, people’s life has closely connections with internet. Even though sometimes it is hard for us to notice, but the internet is filled in every corner of our lives.

This website is for recording the internet in various forms in our life and especially those, which are easy to be ignored by people.

Digital Billboard

As technological development, more and more traditional commercial operation has been redefined by internet, especially advertising. There are more and more merchants has start to use digital billboard instead of traditional advertising for advertising, due to the flexibility. People can use a computer to control it via internet, they even can use WIFI to connect the billboard with their computer, which allow them to edit the content.

Antenna

There is always been a stereotype towards antenna, that people think the antenna can only be used for receiving the television’s signal. In the fact, as the technology development, it not only can receive the signal for television, but also can signal of internet that can provided network television services to people.

ATM Machine

To people who just want to have some simple deals with bank, for example deposit and withdraw small amount money, using ATMs will always be the first choice to them. All the ATMs are connecting with a host processor, which is an internet service provider. The internet service allows ATM machine to provide timely services to customer and update their back account information with the host processor.

Power Line

Power line is one of the most basic infrastructures in the modern society. It can supply electricity to every electronic products in our home, that almost all of the products, which can connect with internet are required electricity supply.
